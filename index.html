Prompt Hub Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

Prompt Hub is a Flutter application operated by Prompt Hub Team. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Prompt Hub mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We gather the following types of information to deliver core functionality:

  • Admin/Sub-Admin Account Information: Name, email address, and password collected when administrators or sub-admins sign in via Firebase Authentication. The consumer-facing Prompt Hub experience does not require regular users to create accounts.
  • User-Generated Content: Prompts, favorites, and profile details you create or modify within the app.
  • Administrative Records: Sub-admin assignments, roles, and activity logs managed by administrators.
  • Device & Usage Data: Basic usage metrics, crash logs, and device information collected through Firebase services to improve performance.
  • Media Files: Images uploaded via the prompt management tools (stored through ImageBB or similar services).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users and secure access to admin and sub-admin dashboards.
  • Store and manage AI prompts, categories, and featured content.
  • Enable favorites, profile customization, and personalized experience.
  • Monitor activity to prevent unauthorized access and maintain service integrity.
  • Enhance the app through analytics, troubleshooting, and crash diagnostics.
  • Communicate with you, including support requests and account updates.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase, Firestore, Firebase Authentication, and ImageBB, solely to support app functionality.
  • Legal Compliance: When required to comply with applicable laws or respond to valid legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: If Prompt Hub is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred in accordance with privacy laws.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards—including Firebase security rules, access controls, and encrypted network communication—to protect your data. However, no mobile or online service is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account information by contacting us.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account information by contacting support.
  • Request removal of prompts or content you no longer wish to share.
  • Disable notifications or email communication preferences.
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking where supported.

7. International Users

Prompt Hub is operated from Pakistan. If you access the app from outside Pakistan, your information may be transferred, processed, and stored in Pakistan or other countries where we or our service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

8. Children's Privacy

Prompt Hub is not directed to individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notices or by updating the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after revisions constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
